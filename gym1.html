Our Story

BUILT FOR
THE ELITE

Agni Fitness was founded in 2016 with one blazing ambition — to build India's most formidable premium gym experience. Named after the Sanskrit word for fire, we believe every great transformation begins with an inner flame.

From Dharavi to Dadar, athletes, executives and everyday warriors walk through our doors to push beyond limits. Our state-of-the-art facility spans 18,000 sq ft across South Mumbai, with international-grade equipment and expert trainers who deliver results — not excuses.
