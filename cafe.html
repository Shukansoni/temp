Our Story

Where passion
meets the craft

Born from a singular obsession with exceptional coffee, AURUM was founded in 2018 by master roasters who believed that extraordinary coffee deserved extraordinary attention. We travel to origin, forge relationships with farmers, and bring each bean's unique story to life in every cup.

🫘

Fresh-Roasted Beans

Roasted in-house weekly in small batches, ensuring peak freshness and complexity in every brew.

☕

Artisan Brewing

Our baristas train for 200+ hours, mastering every method from precision espresso to pour-over.

🏡

Cozy Atmosphere

A sanctuary designed for makers, thinkers, and dreamers — warm light, soft acoustics, genuine warmth.
