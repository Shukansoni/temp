Our Heritage

A Legacy of Timeless Indian Grandeur

Nestled amidst royal landscapes, Ananta Palace is a celebrated icon of Indian luxury hospitality. Since 1998, we have welcomed discerning travellers from across the world, offering an experience that seamlessly weaves ancient Indian culture with the finest contemporary comforts.

Every corridor tells a story. Every suite is a sanctuary. Our philosophy is simple — to honour every guest with the warmth of an Indian household and the precision of world-class service.
