A Legacy of Flavor

At Ananta Roastery, we believe that every cup tells a story. Sourced directly from high-altitude Indian estates, our beans are carefully handpicked and artisanally roasted to bring out complex, native flavor profiles.

Step into our warm, architecturally curated space where modern luxury meets traditional hospitality. Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a long afternoon with a book, you belong here.
