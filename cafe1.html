Our Heritage

Born from the Heart
of Indian Tradition

Chai Mahal was founded with a single dream — to bring the authentic warmth of India's beloved tea culture into a modern, luxurious setting. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai's Bandra district, we transform centuries-old brewing traditions into extraordinary sensory experiences.

Every cup we serve is crafted with masala sourced directly from Kerala spice farms, tea leaves hand-picked from Darjeeling's misty gardens, and milk from trusted local dairies — because only the finest ingredients deserve to carry the name Chai Mahal.
